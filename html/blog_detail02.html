WFP OUTREACH PROGRAM 2016

Welcome Finance Philippines recently held an outreach program entitled “The Gift of Smile” at Concordia Children’s Services at Sta. Mesa, Manila. With the help of the loving family and community of the said orphanage, WFP management and team was able to deliver a very enthusiastic and fun filled activities. Before the program started WFP President Mr. Suk Sub Shim and other heads was directed to visit all the kids in the orphanage, they met and learned some of the past stories how the orphans get there.

Meanwhile the kids’ ages 7-12 prepared a heartwarming presentation for the visitors that made the WFP employees appreciated the moment, the kids also participated and enjoyed all the games organized for them.

After the enjoyable activities for the kids, WFP Finance Head Mr. Jae Bong Jang hand over a financial assistance in support to Concordia Children’s Services accepted by its coordinator Ms. Hazel Juanico, followed by gift giving activity for the kids from the management and team.

The event ended by distributing a free meal for all the kids and staff of the orphanage that made everyone satisfied and contented.
