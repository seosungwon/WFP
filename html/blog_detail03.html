BAGUIO Branch Opening

As part of the vision to become one of the leading consumer finance companies here in the Philippines, Welcome Finance Philippines (WFP) opens its third branch in Baguio City - the Philippines’ Summer Capital. The objective of WFP is to expand its business coverage territory to be able to serve more customers across North Luzon Region particularly in Benguet, Pangasinan, La Union, Mountain Province, Apayao and other nearby provinces. WFP is dedicated to provide world-class financial solutions to prospective clients which is in line with Welcome Financial Group of Korea to become a globally competitive financial consumer company.

WFP opened its third branch last February 17, 2017 and started off with the blessing of Rev. Fr. Tambic, followed by the ribbon cutting ceremony headed by WFP President Mr. Suk Sub Shim and WFP Operations Head Mr. Jin Chul Kang, Sales & Marketing Head Mr. Michael Tandoc and Baguio Branch Manager Mr. Marcelino Domingo Jr.

President Shim expressed his appreciation and excitement during the branch opening ceremony and confidently shared to them the new loan products of WFP particularly in Secured Loan that will cater the needs of the market. He also shared how WFP plans expand further in the coming years by opening new branches in Cebu City and Quezon City. Mr. Shim ended his speech by expressing his gratitude to those who attended and looking forward to the upcoming achievements and plans of WFP in the country in the years to come. To end the event, Mr. Domingo expressed his commitment to ensure the success of the branch and welcome all customers and partners to visit our branch. He ended the event by introducing the Baguio team and expressed his utmost appreciation to all who attended the said event.
