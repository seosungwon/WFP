You privacy rights as a data subject

- Right to be informed We are bound to furnish you this Privacy Notice to inform you about your data privacy rights, how we process your nonpublic personal information, and how we restrict your nonpublic personal information from unauthorized access.

- Right to object You have a right to object verbally, whether in person or through a phone call, to the processing of your nonpublic personal information, including for direct marketing, automated processing or profiling. You may also withhold your consent to the processing in case of changes or any amendment to the information you declared with us.

- Right to access You may request from us a copy of any information about you that we have in our computer database and/or manual filing system.

- Right to rectification You have a right to dispute the inaccuracy or error in your information on our database and have them corrected immediately and accordingly by accomplishing the Client Data Change Request Form prescribed by our Company.

- Right to erasure or blocking You may exercise your right to suspend, withdraw or order the blocking, removal or destruction of you nonpublic personal information from our database and/or manual filing system upon discovery and substantial proof of any of the following: - Your personal information is incomplete, outdated, false, or unlawfully obtained; - Your personal information is being used for purpose you did not authorize; - Your personal information is no longer necessary for the purposes for which they are collected; - You withdraw consent or object to the processing, and there is no longer legal ground or overriding interest for the processing; - The personal information concerns private information that is prejudicial to you, unless justified by freedom of speech, of expression, or of the press or otherwise authorized; - The processing is unlawful; or - We violated your data privacy rights.

- Right to damages You may be indemnified for any damages you sustained due to inaccurate, incomplete, outdated, false, unlawfully obtained or unauthorized use of your personal information, taking into account any violation of your rights and freedoms as a data subject.

- Right to data portability You may request from us a copy of your personal information in an electronic or structured format that is commonly used and allows you for further use.

- Right to file a complaint You may file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission against us through https://privacy.gov.ph/complaints-main/ if you feel that your nonpublic personal information has been misused, maliciously disclosed, or improperly disposed, or that any of your data privacy rights has been violated: Provided, that you have given us an opportunity in good faith to comply with any legal obligations involving data protection and privacy.
