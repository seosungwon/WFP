CEBU Branch Opening

It's been almost a year since Welcome Finance Philippines (WFP) pursued its bold vision to expand their business coverage to be able to serve the Filipino Market and help bridge their financial needs by offering world-class services. WFP first targeted to open shop in Central Luzon with it's San Fernando Branch in Pampanga followed Sta. Rosa Branch in Laguna for South Luzon in 2016. At the start of 2017, WFP opened it's door to North Luzon by inaugurating the Baguio Branch in Benguet Province last March.

To further expand its business network, WFP set its sight to go out of the Luzon and widen its reach to the Visayas market by opening its 4th branch in Cebu City. The said branch opened last June 7, 2017 located at the Mezzanine Floor of the Trends Plaza in F. Ramos St., Cebu City. The WFP Cebu City Branch opening and blessing was graced by Rev. Fr. Elson Singson, followed by the ribbon cutting ceremony headed by WFP President Suk Sub Shim Division Head, Operations - Jin Chul Kang, Sales and Marketing Head Michael Tandoc, and Cebu Branch Manager Beauty Yusingco.

President Shim welcomed their Cebuano partners by expressing his gratitude and shared how Welcome Finance Philippines has grown since the 2014. He also shared the future plan of the company to assure that WFP is committed to expand its reach and provide the best financial services to the Filipino Market. He also added that WFP has plans of opening more branches in the Visayas region and also excited to open also in the Mindanao region specifically in Davao City in 2018. President Shim ended his speech by sharing the success and advantages of the Sprinter System which is the first Online Application for Sales Agency partners and how it will help WFP meet its goal to become the leading consumer finance company in the country.To end the event, Ms. Yusingco also expressed her excitement to help Cebuanos to provide the best service to customers and partners which is WFP is known for. She also confidently assured all the attendees that she and the rest of her team are ready to serve and provide the people of Cebu a service beyond compare.
